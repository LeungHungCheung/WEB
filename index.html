Biography

Leung Hung Cheung, born in Hong Kong, China, is an undergraduate student of creative media at City University of Hong Kong (CityU).
He first discovered his interest in art during his time at the Hong Kong Community College (HKCC).
He is attracted by the beauty and diversity of the world in photography and films.
This opportunity led him to decide to explore art more deeply by studying at an art school for his bachelor's degree.

Education

Before Leung Hung Cheung's art journey started, he had experienced failure in the HKDSE. To fix this career mistake,
he tried his best to perform in the associate degree in engineering at HKCC from 2021 to 2023. During the period of his associate degree,
he was awarded to the dean's list for one semester and used his high GPA to gain admission to the university.
This experience shaped his understanding in the fields of engineering and mathematics, including electronic circuits,
complex logical thinking, and physics. For now, he is studying hard at the School of Creative Media at CityU,
aiming to produce excellent artwork that benefits society.

Artistic Practice

Leung Hung Cheung's artwork consistently employs an approach rooted in everyday life.
To find the tiny things in public's daily life and express his subjective idea about those phenomena. From micro to macro.
His primary exploration themes will be related to technology, such as QR codes and social media trends.
His process of creating artwork often begins with the tiny and everyday things from his personal life.
And followed by some in-depth research by scholars to identify the critical issue.
